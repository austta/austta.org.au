# Sydney Buses (STA)

## Notes

-   Timetables for regular routes are available on the internet at
    [www.sta.nsw.gov.au](http://www.sta.nsw.gov.au/) while the T-way
    timetable is available in PDF format on the T-way and 131500
    websites.
-   No printed timetables are issued for: school services; sports
    services.
-   E and X routes are express, L Limited Stops - not used on routes 200
    or 400, or 2xx series routes.
-   Some timetables may be internet only and this is shown in the
    individual entries.
-   The Sydney Buses website now has a facility that enables users to
    download and print timetables and maps for all STA school services.\
    The facility is at the following page: 
    <http://www.sydneybuses.info/schools> 

***For information on Sydney Bus Regions see
<http://www.bus.org.au/index.php?title=Bus_Regions_-_New_South_Wales>***
